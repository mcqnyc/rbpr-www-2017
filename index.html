philosophy

The Red Branch team is comprised of public relations professionals who bring a wealth of international experience from the worlds of media, advertising, business, technology, education, entertainment, and fashion.

 

Like many of our clients we are entrepreneurs as well, and we understand and experience the thrills and pitfalls of being in business. We believe this helps us to relate to them and to be as creative in how we philosophy our work as they do theirs, and we will give you the hands–on attention you need to make your business thrive.

 

We value our relationships with our clients, and support their campaigns and businesses as passionately as if they were our own so our agency has made a choice to favor those deeper relationships over a large client roster.

 

That's the difference with Red Branch PR: every member of the team knows that relationships have been and always will be the foundation of our agency, and that our client accounts are not simply numbers, rather they represent visions.
